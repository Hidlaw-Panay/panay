Vice Mayor Arminda Asis-Bermejo

Vice Mayor brief detail

Born on July 11, 1953 at manila to Parents Commissioner Luis R. Asis and Former Mayor Estrella Asis, both deceased and both have been mayors of Panay, Capiz.

She finished her elementary education at Panay Central School in 1965, her Secondary Education at Philippine Women's University in 1969 and her College Education from Colegio dela Purisima Concepcion for the degree of Commerce major in Accounting.

She is married to Jonnel Bermejo, a Secondary School Teacher at Commissioner Luis R. Asis National High School, Pany, Capiz with whom she had 6 children, namely:

  • Dr. Joam Bermejo Malapajo Practitioner of Medicine at Makati Medical Hospital
  • Jason Bermejo who is nurse at the states
  • Jacqueline Bermejo McIver based in Dubai
  • Jennifer Bermejo Acevedo a businesswoman
  • John Justin Bermejo a nurse at Centrum Hospital
  • And Jasper Bermejo a multi-media artist at ABS-CBN
